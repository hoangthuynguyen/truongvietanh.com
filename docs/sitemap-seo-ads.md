# Sitemap Structure Cho truongvietanh.com

Ngay cap nhat: 2026-03-24

Muc tieu cua sitemap moi:
- ro intent cua tung nhom trang
- de scale tu 10 trang tru cot len 200-500 trang ma khong vo kien truc
- dung cho SEO, local SEO, Ads landing va funnel tuyen sinh
- phan biet ro trang production, trang utility va trang noi bo

## 1. Nguyen tac xay sitemap

- Moi URL chi phuc vu 1 intent chinh.
- Moi nhom trang phai co owner, CTA chinh, KPI chinh.
- Slug viet thuong, khong dau, dung dau `-`, tranh emoji va slug mo ho.
- Trang pillar dung de rank + route traffic, khong dung thay landing paid.
- Trang landing paid dung de chuyen doi, khong dung thay pillar.
- Trang campus dung de tao local trust, khong copy mo ta giong nhau.
- Trang blog dung de support authority, khong duoc bien thanh content farm.
- Trang noi bo va trang mau phai noindex, khong dua vao production sitemap.

## 4. XML sitemap nen chia nhu the nao

Khong nen co 1 file `sitemap.xml` qua lon va tron lan moi thu. Nen tach theo nhom:

- `/sitemap.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/core.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/levels.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/campuses.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/local.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/admissions.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/tuition.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/events.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/blog.xml`
  - `/sitemaps/news.xml`

Khong dua vao XML sitemap production:
- `/mau/*`
- `/mau-template/*`
- `/samples/*`
- `/api/health.json`
- thank-you pages neu dang noindex
- route noi bo, route test, route staging

## 5. Internal linking rules toi thieu

- Homepage phai tro toi 4 pillar pages, admissions hub, tuition hub, campus hub.
- Moi pillar phai tro toi campus pages, hoc phi cap hoc, FAQ cap hoc, 5-10 cluster pages.
- Moi campus page phai tro ve pillar lien quan, local landing, tham quan truong, hoc phi.
- Moi local page phai tro toi campus that, pillar that, tuition/admissions page.
- Moi blog page phai tro ve pillar, 1 CTA page, 2 bai lien quan.
- Moi comparison / objection page phai tro toi hoc phi, admissions, campus, pillar lien quan.

## 8. Ket luan

Sitemap moi cua truongvietanh.com nen duoc nhin nhu mot he thong gom 4 tang:

- Tang 1: Home + Brand + Pillars
- Tang 2: Campus + Admissions + Tuition
- Tang 3: Local + Performance + Event
- Tang 4: Blog + FAQ + Comparison + Retention

Neu mot URL moi duoc tao ra, can tra loi 5 cau hoi truoc khi publish:

- URL nay thuoc nhom nao?
- Intent chinh cua no la gi?
- CTA chinh la gi?
- No nhan link tu dau?
- No tro link sang dau?

Neu khong tra loi duoc 5 cau hoi do, chua nen dua URL vao sitemap production.
